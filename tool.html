About Toolbar

Toolbar (formerly One Page Tools) is your one-stop solution for all PDF-related tasks. We provide a suite of 27 free online PDF tools that run entirely in your browser. This means your files never leave your device, ensuring maximum privacy and security.

Why Use Our Tools?

  • Privacy First: No server uploads. All processing happens locally on your computer.
  • Completely Free: No hidden costs or premium subscriptions.
  • No Installation: Works on Windows, Mac, Linux, and mobile devices instantly.

Core Features

Merge & Split: Combine multiple documents into one or extract specific pages.

Compress: Reduce file size for easy email sharing.

Convert: Transform PDFs to Word, Excel, PowerPoint, or Images, and vice versa.

Security: Sign documents digitally or watermark them to protect your intellectual property.
